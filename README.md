# CraftSpace

> A mobile app + smart studio board that connects beginner crafters to guided tutorials, equipment reservations, and live workshops — so you spend less time figuring out and more time making.

🌐 **[Launch the app → crafting-space.vercel.app](https://crafting-space.vercel.app/)**

---

## About

CraftSpace is a concept product designed for beginner crafters who want to learn hands-on skills in a studio environment without the intimidation. The app connects your phone to a studio smart board, so when you walk in, your workspace is already set up for the craft you chose.

Built as a final project for **CSE 440: Human-Computer Interaction** at the **University of Washington Allen School**, Winter 2026.

---

## Features

- **Craft Browser** — Browse and filter across four crafts: Crocheting, Knitting, 3D Printing, and Painting
- **Beginner-Friendly Tutorials** — Step-by-step guides tailored to first-timers
- **Equipment Reservation** — Reserve tools before you arrive so your station is ready
- **Studio Check-In** — Scan a QR code at the studio to sync your phone with the smart board
- **Live Class Enrollment** — Sign up for expert-led in-person workshops directly in the app
- **Progress Tracking & Badges** — Track your skills and earn badges as you level up
- **Mentor Connections** — Connect with instructors and peers in the studio

## Research & Design Process

This project was grounded in user research before any design decisions were made.

- **21 user interviews** with young adults (ages 18–25)
- **Contextual inquiry** at The MILL makerspace
- **Key findings:**
  - 90% of beginners learn at home through online tutorials due to intimidating shared spaces
  - Lack of structure and peer support are the top barriers to getting started
  - Crowding, long wait times, and inconsistent tools create friction in makerspaces
- **Paper prototyping** → **Digital mockups (Figma)** → **Interactive prototype**
